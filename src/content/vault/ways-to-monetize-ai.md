# 35+ Ways to Monetize AI: Strategies & Tactics

> *SI Nexus Brand Voice: Every strategy below is distilled to its operational core. No fluff. Execute or skip.*

## Tier 1 — Digital Product Engines

### 1. AI-Generated eBooks
**Output:** 10-60 page PDFs on niche topics  
**Stack:** ChatGPT (outline + chapters) → Canva (design) → Gumroad (distribution)  
**Revenue Model:** One-time sale ($9–$49), bundle upsells  
**Time to Launch:** 48–72 hours

### 2. Prompt Packs
**Output:** Curated, tested prompt libraries (50–500 prompts per pack)  
**Stack:** GPT-4 + systematic testing → Notion/PDF → Gumroad/Etsy  
**Revenue Model:** Tiered pricing ($7–$97), recurring subscription  
**Advantage:** Zero COGS, infinite scalability

### 3. Canva Template Bundles
**Output:** AI-designed social media, business, or resume templates  
**Stack:** ChatGPT (design concepts) → Canva (production) → Etsy  
**Revenue Model:** $5–$30 per bundle, passive income flywheel

### 4. AI-Written Email Sequences
**Output:** Done-for-you sales/onboarding email sequences  
**Stack:** GPT-4 + customer brief → deliverable in 2 hours  
**Revenue Model:** Service ($150–$1500 per sequence) or packaged product

### 5. Micro-Course Creation
**Output:** 3–7 lesson courses on high-demand AI skills  
**Stack:** ChatGPT (curriculum) → Loom (recording) → Gumroad/Teachable  
**Revenue Model:** $27–$197, recurring cohort launches

## Tier 2 — Service Arbitrage

### 6. AI Copywriting Agency
**Output:** Ads, landing pages, product descriptions  
**Stack:** GPT-4 + brand voice guide → Fiverr/Upwork/cold outreach  
**Revenue Model:** $50–$500/project, package retainers  
**Efficiency Gain:** 10x output speed vs. manual writing

### 7. AI SEO Content Service
**Output:** Long-form SEO articles (1500–5000 words)  
**Stack:** SurferSEO + ChatGPT → client CMS  
**Revenue Model:** $100–$500/article, 10+ article monthly retainers  
**Leverage:** Write 10 articles in the time it takes to write 1

### 8. Social Media Management via AI
**Output:** 30 days of posts, captions, and hashtags  
**Stack:** ChatGPT + Buffer/Hootsuite scheduling  
**Revenue Model:** $500–$2000/month per client  
**Scale:** Manage 5–10 clients simultaneously

### 9. AI Video Scriptwriting
**Output:** YouTube, TikTok, and Reels scripts  
**Stack:** ChatGPT (script) → client records → client posts  
**Revenue Model:** $30–$150 per script, bulk packages

### 10. Business Plan Generation
**Output:** Full business plans (executive summary, financials, market analysis)  
**Stack:** ChatGPT + financial modeling prompts  
**Revenue Model:** $300–$800/plan on Fiverr, Upwork, or direct
